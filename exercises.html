Neck

Forced Neck Pull

  1. place palm of right hand on the left side of your head just above the ear
  2. slowly pull your head towards the right shoulder
  3. repeat for your left arm
  4. do this 3 times per side

Upper Spine

Upper Back Spine Roller

  1. fold a large towel along it's short side
  2. roll up the towel into a cylinder
  3. lie with your back on the floor
  4. place the rolled up towel just below your armpits, on the floor
  5. interlock your fingers and hold your hands behind your head
  6. arch your back as you suck your belly button inwards
  7. hold for 5 seconds
  8. repeat, 5 times, each time moving the towel closer to your neck

Shoulder Blade Squeeze

  1. stand up tall with your arms in a praying position in front of you
  2. slowly squeeze your shoulder blades backwards, moving your arms to the side as you squeeze
  3. repeat 5 times

Lower Spine

Child's Pose

  1. kneel down on the floor
  2. slowly sit back, moving your buttocks towards your heels
  3. at the same time, lean your outstretched arms forwards until your palms are on the floor
  4. hold for 30 seconds
  5. repeat twice
